This is an educational visualization, not a technical specification. Some details are simplified:

  • Word selection: Real BIP39 generates random entropy bits first, then encodes them as words ‚Äî not picked one-by-one like a slot machine.
  • Brute force time: Doesn't account for current limitations (key derivation) nor future quantum breakthroughs ‚Äî real attacks are much slower today, but quantum computing is a factor to prepare for.
  • Passphrase: BIP39 supports an optional passphrase ("25th word") for additional security, not shown here.
  • Blockchain variations: Different blockchains may have different implementations ‚Äî some don't use a checksum word, others use different word lists or derivation paths.
